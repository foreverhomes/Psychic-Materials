Thank You

Marcel Ramagnano (my hero), Kieran Adams (my confidant), Jeff DeButte, Heather Kirby, Ryan Carley, Gavin Gardiner, Anissa Hart and Jenny Mecija.

Karen Campos, May Troung and Sammy Rawal.

Mecija family, Dyer family, Charlotte and Elliot, Santo Nino family, Kapisanan Philippine Centre, JoAnne Dubois, Sean O'Neil, and Ohbijou.

Hannah Dyer, this engine, this desire...for you.

For all uncompromisingly queer things.
